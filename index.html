About Gap India

Specialized recruitment for IT & NonIT roles across India

Founded in 2023, Gap India is a dynamic recruitment solutions provider based in New Delhi. We specialize in both IT and nonIT hiring, offering endtoend talent acquisition services to clients in technology, healthcare, manufacturing, BFSI, retail, logistics, education and more.

Our strength lies in understanding the unique hiring needs of each sector and designing search strategies that connect the right talent with the right opportunity. Whether it is a leadership mandate, a critical niche hire or highvolume recruitment, our team is focused on speed, quality and longterm fit.

Dedicated practice teams for IT, NonIT and leadership hiring.
Structured screening, interviewing and background validation.
Transparent process, realtime updates and accountable SLAs.
Partnership mindset focused on repeat and longterm relationships.
Peoplefirst, performancedriven. Management and consultants with national and international exposure across finance, consumer, manufacturing, healthcare, technology, engineering, automotive and banking.
